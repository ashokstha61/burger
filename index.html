About Us

Welcome to Berger Hut, where the beauty of nature meets the comfort of home. Nestled in the serene mountains, our hut offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and modern amenities. Whether you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life or embark on an adventurous outdoor excursion, Berger Hut is your ideal destination.

Founded in 1995, our hut has been a sanctuary for travelers seeking peace and adventure. We pride ourselves on our rich history, welcoming atmosphere, and commitment to sustainability. Join us and experience the tranquility and beauty of the Alps like never before.
